Taj Mahal


The beautiful white marble palace made by Mughal Emperor , Shah Jahan in 1632 A.D. and is one of the seven wonders in the world . It is located in Agra . Check out this website about Taj Mahal tourism . (Click here )


The Red Fort


The Red Fort was also made by Shah Jahan . It took 8 years to complete . It is located in the Indian capital , Delhi . Every year on the Indian Indepence Day (August 15) the Indian flag is hoisted by the Prime Minister on top of the Red Fort (Lal Qila in Hindi).
